Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I buy a paid owner package?

Paid advertisement significantly improves the visibility of your listing in the search results which increases the chances of getting more responses, as compared to a free listing. Increased Visibility! More Responses! Faster Closure! The key features of our paid subscriptions are: 1- Preferential Visibility in Search 2- Verified Tag with Photoshoot & Videography* 3- Dedicated Relationship Manager, Filtered Responses on Assist & Assist Plus packages

What will the Relationship Manager do?

hunthouse offers a personalized advertisement management service called Assist & Assist Plus, where an experienced Relationship Manager manages your property advertisement, contacts leads on your behalf, filters potential buyers/tenants, and connects you with the best prospects.

What is Digital Marketing Campaign for Owners?

The key features of Digital Marketing Campaign for Owners are: 1- Targeting of interested buyers/tenants on Facebook and Google Display Network 2- Eye-catchy property advertisement banners designed using latest technology 3- Perfect blend of branding and lead generation

Are there any hidden charges in Paid Owner Packages?

There are no hidden charges in Paid Owner Packages. hunthouse does not charge any brokerage or commission. You only pay for the Subscription charges for the selected plan.
